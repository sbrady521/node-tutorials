Streams and Buffers:
====================


Buffers: 
========

	Temporary holding spot of data being moved from one place to another.

	Intentionally limited in size.

	Gathering data and putting in a Temporary spot to be processed as it comes.
	

Stream:
=======

	A sequence of data made available over time.

	Pieces of data eventually combine into a whole.

	Imagine something similar to a river where water flows down it.


	data is flowing down stream.

	data being passed from one comp to another.

	Movie streaming is done as its downloading as it comes.

	Watching the video as it is being downloaded.
